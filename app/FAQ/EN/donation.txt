Q:
What is the fund?
A:
The Yemen Charity Fund (YCF) aims to assist underprivileged students who are unable to pay tuition fees, as well as students facing financial difficulties that could lead to legal consequences. The fund helps them get out of their financial crises, enabling them to continue their studies. It is directly linked to the official UTM SMART application, and all donations go to the fund's account at the university. You can learn more by visiting our official website: https://bit.ly/3nNfQ5E
